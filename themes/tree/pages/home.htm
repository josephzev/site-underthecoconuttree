Under the Coconut Tree Beach Homestay

Featuring a private path which leads to An Bang Beach, Under The Coconut Tree Hoi An Homestay Hoi An Homestay offers private and dormitory rooms with coconut thatched roofs and free WiFi. Located within 50 m of a morning market in Danang, Under The Coconut Tree Hoi An Homestay Hoi An Homestay is 1 km from Cua Dai public beach and 4 km from Hoi An Market. Marble Mountains is 13.1 km away while Da Nang International Airport is 21 km from the homestay.

    Location

    The beach has rapidly grown from a sleepy beach side village, now thriving and becoming its own destination. An Bang Beach became popular so quickly, that the area is not very built up at all, adding to its beach village feel. The bars and restaurants are all quite small, but they definitely do not fault when it comes to delicious food, refreshing drinks and entertainment. There are substantial amounts of money being put into An Bang, attracting even more holiday makers to the area. An Bang Beach is well set up for tourists. There are a few ATMs, pharmacies, convenience stores, clothing and swimming accessory shops. Coupled with lots of bars, restaurants, hotels, villas, homestays, spas, massage salons, and even cooking classes.
